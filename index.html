Chef's Word

Our Story

Carpathian Garden began with a simple idea: the best meal in Romania shouldn't need a stiff white tablecloth to feel special. Just good light, good company, and ingredients that traveled less than a mile to reach your plate.

Chef Kevin Luo trained in Lyon and Tokyo before coming home to the mountains above Alba Iulia, and every dish here still carries that journey: French technique, Japanese restraint, and the wild garlic, mushrooms, and trout that grow right outside our kitchen door. We built our terrace into the hillside rather than against it, so every table looks out over the same forest that feeds it.

Kevin Luo
